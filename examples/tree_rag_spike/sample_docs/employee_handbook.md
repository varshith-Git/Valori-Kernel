# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook explains the policies that apply to all
full-time employees. Keep it handy — it is the source of truth for how we work.

## 1. Working Hours and Remote Work

This section covers when and where you are expected to work.

### 1.1 Core Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. Core hours during
which all employees must be reachable are 10:00 to 16:00. Outside core hours you
may flex your start and end times by up to two hours.

### 1.2 Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Working remotely for more
than 3 days per week requires written approval from your manager. Fully remote
arrangements are decided case by case and must be renewed every 12 months.

## 2. Leave and Time Off

Acme offers several distinct categories of leave. Each has its own allowance.

### 2.1 Annual Leave

Every full-time employee is entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per
calendar year, in addition to public holidays. Up to 5 unused annual leave days
may be carried over into the following year.

### 2.2 Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. A doctor's note is required for
any sick absence longer than 3 consecutive days. Unused paid sick days do not
carry over and are not paid out on departure.

### 2.3 Parental Leave

New parents are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave, which may be taken
any time within the first 12 months after birth or adoption. Parental leave is
separate from, and does not reduce, your annual leave allowance.

## 3. Compensation and Benefits

This section describes how and when you are paid, and your benefits.

### 3.1 Salary and Payroll

Salaries are paid monthly, on the last working day of each month. Payslips are
available in the employee portal. Overtime is not paid for salaried roles but is
compensated with time off in lieu.

### 3.2 Health Insurance

Acme covers 100% of the premium for employee health insurance and 50% of the
premium for dependents. Coverage begins on your first day of employment.

### 3.3 Retirement Plan

Acme matches employee retirement contributions up to 6% of base salary.
Contributions vest after two years of continuous service.

## 4. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to uphold the following standards.

### 4.1 Confidentiality

Confidential information must not be shared outside the company. This obligation
continues for 2 years after your employment ends.

### 4.2 Expense Policy

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days with a valid receipt.
Expenses over 200 USD require pre-approval from your manager.
